Vault Content

Cadmium will integrate Vault content into your content when it loads. It also polls Vault for changes, causing the content to rerender when a change is detected.

Adding Vault Content

Adding vault content is very simple with Cadmium. Just add a div with the identifier of the content you want to inline.

<div data-vault-guid="UUID">DEV CONTENT</div>

An Example

<div data-vault-guid="01c571c4-0fdf-4b3c-896d-0feda89adc9c">Bah</div>
