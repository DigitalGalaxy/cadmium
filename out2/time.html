Time Savings

Currently, we spend a lot of time doing deployments. Here are some very rough figures:

  1. We do 10-15 internal QA deploys for every production release. These take 15-20 minutes when things go well.
  2. We usually do one gene staging deployment per production release These take 30-60 minutes when things go well.
  3. Every production deployment takes 1-2 hours, when things go well.
